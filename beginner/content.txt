The Beginner’s Prayer

Well, here I am. I know that much.
What am I thinking? 
Whose idea was this? 
How did I get here?

Well, it’s too late for all that
and anyway I know the answers. 
I’m trying something new
I’m only a beginner
I’m still a beginner
And I’m starting at the start.

One look today, just a look.
One thing I can say
I took a look.
Tomorrow another look 
and something will look the same 
and now it becomes familiar. 
And that’s something I can say.

And looking daily.
And habits making.
And patterns taking
hold of my attention.

Until I say
I want to dive in
I want to try it
I might as well.
So when I dive in, try it, try
Someone help me, watch me, why
Because I’m afraid that I might die
Or crash or get hurt or what if I
Fail? Miss? Mess up? Then I
Can count on you to say, here I am
Take another look,

Another dive, just try.
